Kalamandir Club Card

Welcome to Kalamandir Club

More shopping at Kalamandir means more gifts and more benefits. Kalamandir respects its customers and rewards them by offering special gifts and benefits on special occasions. Among various such steps, the "Kalamandir Club Card" is most popular as through this, a customer is eligible for gifts throughout the year.

Club Card Benefits

  • Club card is issued on presentation of purchase bills worth Rs. 1500/-.
  • On completion of purchase worth Rs. 7500/- or more within 12 months, you are entitled to a gift of fabrics/garments from our range worth 7.5% of your total purchases. The gift must be availed within 30 days from the expiry of the card.

Important Information

Your Kalamandir Club Card is valid for 12 months from the date of issue. Keep track of your purchases and claim your rewards within 30 days of card expiry to enjoy the full benefits of the program.

Ready to Join?

Visit any Kalamandir store with your purchase bills worth Rs. 1500/- and get your Club Card today!
