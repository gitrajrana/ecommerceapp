About Kalamandir

Our Story

Incepted way back in 1949 as "Burma General Stores" today's Kalamandir is a mega shopping experience for people in and around Orissa. Kalamandir a leading fashion house of Eastern India is managed by Bhubaneswar's well-known "The Singh Family". Latest trends in fashion, high quality customer service and its perfect location in the heart of city centre attract both celebrities, tourists and the common people to taste the ever festive mood of Kalamandir Shopping.

'Kalamandir' has been the mentor of exclusive trends in fashion since the last five decades. Style does not simply encompass the ruling trends. It is a complete aesthetic statement. Here in 'Kalamandir' you will find every opportunity to revel in style. Kalamandir is a complete "Family Shop" with separate sections for everyone. Kalamandir offers over 8000 sqft of shopping space, which is aesthetically decorated, and centrally air conditioned for customers' comfort and shopping pleasure.

Kalamandir's strength lies in its variety. It houses clothes for all ages, occasions and sentiments. It is one of the best stores in Eastern India where one can shop for every member of the family a "one-stop shop". Completing more than half a century, 'Kalamandir' has always been successful in bringing smiles on the faces of thousands of its customers. Today, 'Kalamandir' has become Orissa's premier fashion destination stocking the widest range in men's wear, ladies wear, kids wear and furnishings, all under one roof.

Philanthropy

All the three brothers Sardar Gurdayal Singh, Sardar Rajinder Singh, Sardar Tajinder Singh are actively involved in social work through various cultural organisations. After the devastation of the Super Cyclone in October 1999, Mr. Gurdayal Singh was closely involved along with a social organisation from Punjab that set up mass kitchens in Jagatsinghpur and Ersama and fed thousands of people for nearly twenty days.

Kalamandir has been doing community services where it has helped weaker section of the society distributing cycle rickshaws, blankets and saving certificates.

Beyond Kalamandir

The Singh Family has been involved in many projects creating employment opportunity in Bhubaneswar. The group has successfully extended to franchise stores like The Raymond Showroom, Van Heusen / Allen Solly Showroom. "The Royale Midtown" hotel, strategically located in the heart of the city has already made a niche for itself in the rapidly growing hospitality industry of the state.
